Help & Support

For issues with the application or if you need to register as a manager, contact admin@example.com.

General Overview

This application is designed to manage work attendance and leave requests efficiently. It allows users to submit leave requests, view attendance records, and access a calendar for better planning.

Navigating the Application

Use the navigation bar at the top to access different features like submitting leave requests, viewing your attendance records, and managing your calendar.

Submitting Leave Requests

To submit a leave request, navigate to the 'Requests' section, select the type of leave, and choose the start and end dates. Your request will be sent for approval to your manager.

Managing Leave Requests (for Managers)

If you're a manager, you can approve, deny, or cancel leave requests from your team in the 'Manage Requests' section.

Viewing Attendance Records

Your attendance records are available on the homepage. Here you can view your daily attendance status for the current month.

Using the Calendar

The calendar feature allows you to view your attendance and leave requests in a calendar format, helping you plan your schedule better.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter any issues, first try refreshing the page or logging out and back in. For persistent issues, please contact the admin.

FAQs

Here are some answers to frequently asked questions:

  • Can I put two types of events in the calendar? - No, you can only enter one type per day. You should enter the corrosponding type such as 'In Office' or 'Work From Home' for how you spent the majority of your working day.
  • How do I cancel a leave request? - Navigate to the 'Requests' section and click on the 'Cancel' button next to your request.
  • Can I edit a submitted leave request? - Once submitted, a leave request cannot be edited. You need to cancel it and submit a new one. If it has been approved, contact your manager to cancel it on your behalf.
  • Why can't I change Annual Leave, Non Working Day or Flexi Leave in my calendar? - You cannot overwrite an attedence type that required approval. Contact your manager to cancel your leave, once this is done you will be able to edit the dates on the calendar.
